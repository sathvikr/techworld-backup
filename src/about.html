About

What is TechWorld?

TechWorld is a technology event integrating aspects from hackathons, competitive algorithm-oriented competitions, and more.
Events
All events can be found here.
The 2021 TechWorld is split into two different days, each with different events related to technology. Events range from competitive programming to learning from reputable tech speakers, CEOs, and experts in the field.
Prizes
What will the champions get?
Many events at TechWorld have prizes if they are competitive enough. Prizes range from a grand plaque (signifying victory at the most prestigious TechWorld event, the Tech Tourney) to signed certificates of recognition.
