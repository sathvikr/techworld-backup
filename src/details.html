Tech Tourney

What exactly is the Tech Tourney and how can I register?
What is it?
About the Tech Tourney
The Tech Tourney is a two phase tournament testing computer science skills, open to everyone around the world. The first phase will be a contest conducted online on our platform. The 16 best performers will be invited to the second round at TechWorld.
Important Dates
Important dates for the Tech Tourney
The registration deadline for the entire tournament is at the end of the day on 10/04/2021. The test for the first phase will be at 3:00 pm on 11/10/2021. The second phase will be held on Day 1 at the in-person TechWorld event.
Registration Information
How to register for the Tech Tourney
If you have not done so already, the first step is to create a TechWorld account. This will be used to keep track of your scores. The next step is to fill out the form to register for the Tech Tourney itself, which can be found here.

TechSposition

What exactly does this tech exposition entail?
Present a Project
Showcase your creation
TechSposition gives you the amazing opportunity to present your projects to interested people at a tech-specialized school. Note that 50 projects will be selected to present at TechWorld. If your project is selected, please bring the necessary materials to present.
Important Dates
Important dates for the Tech Tourney
The registration deadline for the TechSposition is at the end of the day on 10/04/2021. You will receive an email on 11/04/2021 letting you know whether or not your project has been selected. The actual exposition will be held on Day 2 at the in-person TechWorld event.
Registration Information
How to register for the TechSposition
As for everything TechWorld affiliated, an account is necessary to proceed, so create one if you have not already. Afterwards, fill out this form by 10/04/2021. Again, note that doing so will not gaurantee that your project is selected.

Other Events

Details about other events at TechWorld
Build an App
Station
The objective of this station is to create a web or mobile application in a given time frame. The most interesting or useful application will receive a certificate of recognition. Prerequisites for this involve the programming environment for your chosen language, which can be installed at the event if it hasn't been already.
Typing Tourney
Station
Participants will play a series of games on TypeRacer against each other. The winner of the overall tournament will be the participant who has won the greatest number of games in total. Note that as this is a tournament that is more inclined towards fun relaxation, no prize will be awarded to the winner.
Cipher Challenge
Full event
The Cipher Challenge is a rather unique exploit of both technology and espionage; in this station, participants are tasked with creating a cipher of their own from scratch, with the goal of establishing the most complex system. After a timed cipher cracking session, the individuals with the most solved ciphers win.
Programming 50v50
Full event
Programming 50v50 is perhaps one of the least complex of the TechWorld events. Two massive teams are pitted against each other in a coding war, with puzzles being electronically delivered to both sides. Ranging from simple riddles to complex algorithms, the side with the most problems solved ultimately emerges victorious.
Design Challenge
Full event
We emphasize the importance of creativity in technology. Thus, for our artistic participants, we will be hosting a design challenge based around CAD virtual development and paper based 3d modelling. This challenge will be judged upon a predetermined category, with the most practical but astounding designs crowned as winners.
Game Con
Full event
In order to accompany our interests in technology while simultaneously creating an enjoyable atmosphere, we decided to create GameCon. After a quick introduction from leading game designers, participants have the ability to create a game of their choice and also try out creations of their peers.
