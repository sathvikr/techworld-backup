Why Attend?

TechWorld brings together passion and technology by organizing a broad event accompanying these factors. Attending will allow you to listen to in-person talks from professionals and renowned individuals within the field, showcase your own projects in an exposition, and participate in a multitude of software and hardware based competitions.
